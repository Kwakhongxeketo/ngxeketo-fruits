About Our Fruit Shop

Ngxeketo fruit's is CAPE TOWN based fresh produce supplier and now your online Green Grocer and expanding Food Store. With 2 years experience in the fresh produce industry you can trust us to pick the best on your behalf and deliver it to your doorstep.

Delivery areas - We deliver in and around Cape Town only. Please see the section 'About Ordering' for delivery areas, or if not sure please contact us to check if we deliver to your area.

Payment - Payment is by eft. Please use your NAME & SURNAME (no other information like order number or street name) that is a huge help when reconciling the payment. Also do not round the amount up or down, again for purposes of the reconciliation of payment.

Packaging - we endeavour to add no plastic when packing whole fruit and veg for home delivery (bar items already packaged at point of purchase). Our herbs are now packed in environmentally friendly compostable cellulose packets.
